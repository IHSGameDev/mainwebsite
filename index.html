About Us

  • Learn critical computer science and design skills to create and publish your own games
  • Use languages and softwares like C# with Unity, Adobe Photoshop and Blender.
  • Win competitions and establish yourself within the game development community

Gamers of the world unite! Irvington Game Development Club gives its members the opportunity to develop and test out new video games based on their interests

Learn More

We Offer top of the line resources for anyone who is interested in the game development/design.

Come meet us at room 54 for our first intro meeting Monday 11/1/21, to learn more information about the club and projects/events we host.

  • 01 Would you like to learn about the design aspect of games?

    Our officers are experts who can guide you through the process of design, starting with concept art moving to creating actual maps/3D models in unity.

  • 02 Want to be a Game Programmer?

    We offer courses in C#, Unity, Unreal Engine and even photon server scripts(used to create online games), all of these courses are distributed and taught with no cost to you!

  • 03 What opportunities do we offer?

    Not only do we give the resources required for you to begin game development, we also host events like Game Jams where you can compete with other schools and the entire country. We also bring in game developers/designers from prestigious universities such as CMU, and organizations like RIOT games to give you an overview of the field.

 

What do we offer?

We offer help in these softwares/languages and many more!

C# 100%
Unity 90%
Electronic Art (Blender & Maya) 75%
Adobe Tools 55%
